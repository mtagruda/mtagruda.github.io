Cloudiness




We analyzed and plotted out each city on their cloudiness. In this visualization, we see that a majority of the plot points occur at 0 or 100% cloudiness, with many points being random. While the equator is generally rainy, the cloudiness would be generally be high at 0 degrees latitude. Considering the day or time of year, other factors will also need to be reviewed before any strong conclusions can be made, such as whether hurricanes are brewing in the gulf at the moment.
